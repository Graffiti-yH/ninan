June is an open-source alternative to Pixel Journal, built with Jetpack Compose and Material Design 3. It is designed to be a secure, offline-first multimedia capsule of your life.

Features:
* Multimedia Capsules: Attach photos, videos, and precise locations to entries.
* Tagging Groups: Categorize entries using Spaces, People, and Topics tag groups.
* Soundtrack Support: Paste links from major streaming platforms to fetch cover art and details.
* Mood Tracking: Tag entries with emojis to log your emotional journey.
* Rich Text Editor: Style text with bold, italics, underline, highlight, and tag autocompletion.
* Timeline & Streaks: Navigate history via a Month View calendar timeline and view writing streaks.
* Privacy Vault: Secure entries using Biometric Unlock or custom PIN.
* Expressive Theming: Supports Dynamic Wallpaper Colors (Material You) and custom font selection.
* Total Ownership: 100% offline architecture with local Backup & Restore.
* Cloud Sync: Keep journal in sync across devices using WebDAV (e.g. Nextcloud, ownCloud).
